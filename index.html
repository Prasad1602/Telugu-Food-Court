About Us

TFC Family Restaurant is a vibrant dining destination that celebrates the rich and diverse flavors of authentic Andhra and Telangana cuisine, offering everything from spicy biryanis to traditional sweets. With its warm ambiance and carefully curated menu, TFC brings the true essence of Telugu culinary heritage to food lovers seeking genuine home-style tastes..
